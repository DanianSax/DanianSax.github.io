About Me

Actually, my name is Julian Danieli, a saxophone player and producer hailing from Cordoba, Argentina. Born on October 8, 1996, music has been the heartbeat of my life since I can remember.

Beyond the saxophone, I also thrive in the realm of production. Crafting melodies and harmonies is not just a passion but a way of life for me.

My journey with the saxophone commenced at the age of 16, igniting a flame within me that burns brighter with each passing day. Over the years, I've had the privilege of lending my talents to various musical ensembles spanning an array of genres including rock, jazz, reggae, pop, blues, and R&B, among others. This diverse musical palette has not only enriched my playing but also broadened my artistic horizons.

In addition to live performances, I've ventured into the digital realm, sharing original content and creations on platforms like YouTube. It's a space where I can express myself freely and connect with fellow music enthusiasts on a deeper level.

Curious to discover more? Dive into my portfolio and uncover the layers of my musical journey.
